Dijkstra

March 23

I was inspired to make this visualization tool for Dijkstra's algorithm after learning about the proof in my deterministic operations research class my sophomore year. The pathfinding algorithm searches neighboring nodes, as shown in red, and finds the path bearing the least weight from the starting point to the goal.
