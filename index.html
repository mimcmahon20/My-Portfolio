Snake & Dijkstra

Creating the game Snake was the first CS project I ever completed back in 2018. I wrote it using Java and GUI's specifically JFrames. It took me a few months to figure out all the bugs. After learning about dijkstra's algorithm in my Deterministic Operations Research class, I was inspired to implement it into the game of Snake. I first created a visualization of the algorithm, then the game, then attempted to merge the two.

After many hours of trial and error, I finally was able to get the algorithm implemented smoothly into the game. Currently, the snake will live until either it traps itself, or food spawns at a point it can't see. This is the version you saw above!
